# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

---

## Standards of Behavior
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- Trolling or insults
- Public or private harassment
- The use of sexualized language or imagery

---

## Reporting Issues
Instances of abusive, harassing, or unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the maintainers at [your-email@example.com]. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly.

---

## Enforcement
Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct and may take appropriate action in response to unacceptable behavior.
