# Security Policies and Responsibilities

---

## Reporting Vulnerabilities
If you discover a security vulnerability within this project, please do the following:
1. Contact the maintainers at [security-email@example.com] with the details.
2. Provide enough information to reproduce and validate the issue.

---

## Coordinator
The maintainer team will continuously review for security risks and vulnerabilities. 
We are committed to: 
- Mitigating significant risk as a priority. 
- Communicating details when fixes are released.

Thank you for helping keep the Trading App safe for everyone!
